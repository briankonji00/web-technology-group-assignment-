About Us

The 19th Hole is more than just a place to play golf
its a community of like-minded individuals who loves the game and enjoy time together.

Where Miss Hits Are Just Practice Swings

The 19 Hole is golf place that was founded by Sam Steel, an avid golfer who enjoyed Golf in his youth. It course has over 600 acres of scenic countryside with gently rolling hills, mature hills and a meandering creek. The course features a mix of short and long holes, with numerous water hazards and sand traps to challenge players of all skill levels Our club is known for its fun and friendly social events.

The club hosts several tournaments throughout the year including:
1. The annual championship which attracts members from all over the country
2. The Spring and Fall golf leagues
3. The junior golf tournament which encourages young golfers to develop their skills.
