Types of Membership

1. Full/Eminent Member (Men and Ladies):
  • This category requires payment of the full fee, including all green fees, and grants playing rights to the course at any time.
  • Each member is entitled to one vote at a General Meeting.
  • Eminent Membership is extended to the staff of foreign Embassies in Zimbabwe.


    • 2. Limited Members (Men and Ladies):
  • Members in this category have a handicap and are affiliated with golf unions.
  • They are entitled to play on Monday a.m., Tuesday a.m. and p.m. Wednesday a.m., Thursday a.m. and p.m., Friday a.m. and p.m., Saturday pm and Sunday a.m. and p.m., with no green fee charged.
  • They have no vote at a General Meeting.
  • They are unable to play on Wednesday afternoons and Saturday mornings without paying a green fee.


    • 3. Senior Members (Men and Ladies):
  • This category is available to members aged sixty or older who have been golfing members of the Club for at least five years.
  • Members in this category have a handicap and are entitled to play on Monday a.m. and p.m., Tuesday a.m. and p.m., Wednesday a.m., Thursday a.m., and Friday a.m., as well as Saturday p.m., with no green fee charged.
  • They have no vote at a General Meeting.


    • 4. Service Members (Men and Ladies):
  • Members in this category must be sixty-five years or older and have been golfing members of the Club for at least twenty years.
  • They are entitled to play golf any day of the week except Wednesday p.m. and Saturday a.m., when a green fee is payable.
  • They have no vote at a General Meeting.


    • 5. Junior/Student Member (Men and Ladies)
  • Members in this category are entitled to play golf any day of the week except Wednesday afternoons and Saturday all day, unless approved by the Golf Committee.
  • The age limit for junior Membership is eighteen years or non-university.
  • The same fee is charged for students up to the age of twenty-five years on the production of a valid student card.
  • Members in this category have a handicap and are affiliated with golf unions.
  • They have no vote at a General Meeting.


    • 6. House Member (Men and Ladies):
  • Members in this category do not have playing rights and do not have a vote at a General Meeting.
  • They have access to the clubhouse facilities.
  • They pay a green fee determined by the Golf Committee.


    • 7. Out-of-Country Members (Men and Ladies):
  • Members in this category have no playing rights and pay the full affiliated member green fee.
  • They have no vote at a General Meeting.


    • 8. Honorary Member (Men and Ladies):
  • Members in this category are entitled to play on Monday and Tuesday all day, Wednesday a.m., Thursday a.m., and Friday a.m.
  • The subscription includes all green fees.
  • They have no vote at a General Meeting.
  • Members in this category have a handicap and are affiliated with golf unions.
  • Honorary Membership is extended to Ministers of the Church.


    • 9. Life Member:
  • Life Membership is conferred upon a member who has rendered meritorious service to the Club.
  • The nomination for Life Membership must be approved by the Board of Management.
  • A Life Member has a vote at a General Meeting.


    • 10. Professional Golfing Membership:
  • Members in this category pay the full member fees, including green fees, and have playing rights on the course at any time.
  • They do not pay affiliation levies to the amateur golfing bodies, have a handicap, and are not allowed to participate in club competitions.
  • They have no vote at a General Meeting.


    • 11. Family Member:
  • The husband pays the full fee in the category of his choice.
  • The wife and children pay 50% of the subscription of their categoryâ€™s choice.
  • Once the children are eligible for full or limited Membership, they are no longer entitled to the discount.


    • 12. Corporate Membership:
  • This special category of Membership is determined by the Board at its discretion under terms and conditions commensurate with benefits that accrue therefrom, provided all other criteria for Membership of the Club are met.


    • 13. Pre-Paid LIMITED18, LIMITED24, LIMITED32, and LIMITED48:
  • Members in these categories have full playing rights, subject to tee time availability.
  • These are Fully Pre-Paid services offering either 18, 30, or 48 rounds in a membership year.
  • Half rounds are also permitted. LIMITED18 represents 18 rounds of golf paid fully in advance.
  • LIMITED24 represents 24 rounds of golf paid in advance or quarterly.
  • LIMITED32 represents 32 rounds of golf paid in advance or quarterly.
  • LIMITED48 represents 48 rounds of golf paid in advance or quarterly for members aged 26 to 39 years old ONLY.
  • Family discounts apply for husbands and wives joining together, and children under 18 join for free and also get 48 rounds each.
  • On reaching the age of 19, student members move to a full fee-paying category.
  • Members in all of these categories pay relevant affiliation fees and are entitled to a handicap.
  • Rounds must be used up in a single membership year, which runs from 1st March to the end of February annually.
